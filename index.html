ABOUT ME

A born creative, my career with technology began as a Software Designer, with Advanced Micro Devices. After a 4 year stint with AMD, I was caught up in a round of layoffs. Finding myself out of my comfort zone, I embarked on a very different career path, as a successful jewelry designer. After ten years of working for myself, I became enamored with User Experience Design and Web Development. I decided it was time for a career change and dove head first into General Assembly's User Experience Design Immersive Course (This course was 5 days a week, from 9am - 5pm, for 10 weeks). Shortly after finishing my first course, I completed a second immersive course, this time for Web Development (This course was 5 days a week, 9am - 5:30pm, for 12 weeks). I loved every minute of both courses and embrace every chance I have to utilize my newfound skillsets!
